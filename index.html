Sprouts Salad Recipe Description

Moong sprouts salad is a very easy, tasty as well as a very healthy salad recipe. Low Fat, Gluten Free and Vegan Recipe.Moong sprouts salad is specially good for people who does not have much time for cooking or who does not know much about cooking specially college goers who are studying in a hostel or staying away from family for job or studies. An excellent recipe.

Total time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup steamed Moong and Moth Dal Sprouts
  • 1 cubed Cucumber
  • 1 cubed Tomato with or without skin
  • 1 ripe Avocado sliced
  • 8-10 small cubes of Tofu (some pieces can be crushed too)
  • Boiled Potato cubed optional
  • 2 Green chilies cut finely
  • Roasted Peanuts handful
  • Grapes handful
  • Oil 1 tbsp
  • 1 tbsp Lime juice
  • 1/2 tsp chat masala
  • Table salt to taste

Prepration

  1. Transfer sprouts to a large mixing bowl.

    Time:20 seconds

  2. Add all the ingredients for the salad.

    Time:1 minute

  3. Give it a good toss. Add oil, salt, chat masala and lemon juice.

    Time:1 minute

  4. Mix gently; you can use a fork to mix the salad.

    Time:1 minute

  5. Garnish with Peanuts and Grapes.

    Time:20 seconds

  6. Serve the sprouts salad with a Mint cooler in the afternoon!

    Time:1 minute
